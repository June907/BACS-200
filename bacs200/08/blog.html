The “New Literacy” of the Morden Day

Our society has changed so fast and dramatically since the Internet was invented. Nowadays, the majority of people are so obsessed with their phone, with their social media account, all the social media seems to be the new literacy for the modern days. So among all the social media applications, what are the most popular social media applications, and what makes them so unique?

According to Statista, the top most popular social media are Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter as the three mainstream social media. However, some social media applications from recent years are pretty unique and become the most popular social media in the young generations, like Snapchat and TikTok. Today what I want to talk about is that what makes Instagram, Snapchat and TikTok become so popular in the young generation among hundreds of other social media platforms.

For people that may not familiar with Instagram, Instagram is an American photo and video-sharing social networking service. Compare with the old social media platform like Facebook, Instagram has a more concise user interface when you open the app, you will see other people’s photo, video, or story sharing, also it is pretty easy to use as well. You can share your pictures or your journey through Instagram just by a simple tab, also by clicking the explore tab, you can find out other people’s posts on what you may interest. Since people can share their life through Instagram while other people can view it, Instagram makes the connection between people and people closer.

People like to take pictures, they like to take pictures of their happy moments, their journeys, their life. Because of it, Snapchat came along and become the major way of nowadays generation chatting. Snapchat is a social networking service, Unlike Instagram, Snapchat provided an ability to people can take a snap anywhere and anytime, and they can edit words on their photo and send it to others instead of having all people can view it. Snapchat is more convenient for sharing a moment with others compared with other social media platforms.

Nowadays, people like to get more information in a short time of period, whenever people are waiting in a line, in the airport, they most likely will use their social media to kill time, to see what is interesting. Therefore, TikTok becomes the platform for people that like to demonstrate their unique skills, and also provides a way for people that use social media to kill time. TikTok is a video-sharing social networking service, it really gives more opportunities for young people that like to do singing, dancing, comedy, and lip-syncing, etc. With the convenience of showing your own skills in a few minutes to impress the audience, more and more young people are really into TikTok.
